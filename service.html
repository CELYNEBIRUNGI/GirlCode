Services

Overall, Tubayo aims to instill responsible financial habits, promote savings, and empower young individuals to take control of their financial future.

Savings:
1. Emergency Fund:
Saving money with Tubayo fund helps the you cover unexpected expenses or financial downturns without disrupting your personal or business operations or having to sell your investments at unfavorable prices.
2. Capital Preservation:
Saving funds in low-risk accounts or instruments helps you to protect your capital and maintain a stable financial position, reducing the risk of losses.
3. Financial Security:
Saving provides you with a sense of financial security, ensuring you have a reserve to fall back on during challenging times and minimizing the need for external borrowing.

Investments:
1.Stocks:
Tubayo fund will aid you in investing in stocks to participate in the growth and profitability of established or promising companies, potentially resulting in capital appreciation and dividend income.
2.Bonds:
With Tubayo fund, investing in bonds offers a relatively safe option with regular interest payments, providing the fund with stable income while preserving capital.
3.Mutual Funds:
Not forgetting also that investing in mutual funds provides instant diversification and access to professional management, allowing you as a business or individual to spread your investments across a variety of assets and benefit from expertise in portfolio management.

1.Improved Financial Literacy:
Joining Tubayo Fund you are provided with financial education that equips you or your business with knowledge and skills to make informed financial decisions, enhancing their overall financial literacy in collaboration with Uganda Securities Exchange (USE) which conducts financial education programs, workshops, and seminars to promote understanding of investment opportunities, stock market operations, and financial planning.

2. Access to Investment Opportunities:
Tubayo Fund is enthusiastic about offering financial education often providing insights into various investment options, helping individuals identify and access suitable investment opportunities on investment avenues, such as agriculture, real estate, and technology, enabling members to diversify their investment portfolios.

3. Empowerment for Entrepreneurship:
Financial education with Tubayo Fund equips individuals with knowledge on starting and managing businesses, fostering entrepreneurship and economic empowerment.

4. Improved Retirement Planning:
Tubayo Fund plans to offer financial education often emphasizing the importance of retirement planning, helping individuals prepare for a financially secure future.

5. Financial Inclusion and Empowerment:
Financial education provided by the fund promotes financial inclusion, empowering individuals to access and utilize financial services effectively.

1.Savings Accounts: Secure accounts for members to deposit and save money.
2.Loans and Credit Facilities: Access to funds for various purposes with flexible repayment options.
3.Financial Education and Advisory Services: Guidance and resources to improve financial literacy and make informed decisions.

Community engagement examples offered by Tubayo Fund in Uganda:
1. Financial Literacy Workshops:
Tubayo Fund is optimistic to conduct workshops to educate community members on budgeting, saving, debt management, and investment basics, empowering them with essential financial knowledge and skills.

2. Entrepreneurship Training Programs:
The Fund will provide training programs to support local businesses, covering topics such as business planning, marketing strategies, financial management, and accessing funding, fostering entrepreneurship and economic development in the community.

3. Community Investment Projects:
Tubayo Fund initiates projects that benefit the community, such as funding infrastructure improvements and supporting local schools or healthcare facilities, enhancing the overall well-being of the community.

4. Financial Counseling and Advisory Services:
Tubayo Fund offers personalized financial counseling and advisory services to community members, providing guidance on budgeting, debt management, and tailored investment opportunities, addressing individual financial challenges and goals.
